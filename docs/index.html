Team Website of PhuketList

A social network that connects people to check off items on their bucket list

Project Description

Phuket List is a social network where people can join a community to check off items on their bucket list. A bucket list is a compilation of what someone wants to experience or accomplish before they die.

APIs that could possibly be used for the features and JSON content could include the Yelp API, Foursquare API, ect.

Our objective is to inspire and connect people to live their best life and meet great people.

We plan on accomplishing our objective by providing a web application where users can create and subscribe to bucket list items.

There they can communicate with like minded people, set a date on the calendar, and share their experiences.

A way that Phuket List could potentially make money would be through content advertisting from businesses such as restaurants or tourist attractions.

Instead of saying 'someday', users can get an estimate of what a trip to Italy would cost, and what restaurants or tourist attractions they may be interested in.

For individuals with more humble ambitions, instead of planning a trip to Italy, they can instead begin their bucket quest.

These quest include Finding the best burrito in LA, experience the trendiest coffee shops, or dining at the fanciest restaurants in their local area. These quest provide opportunities for like-minded people to meet and fully experience their city.

Features
(currently just ideas to discuss)

  • Authentication with Firebase
  • Use FourSquare or Yelp API for activity/event suggestions
  • Enable subscriptions to Bucket List items (join group)
  • Enable finding of Friends based on similar bucket list items
  • Enable ability to send messages user to users. (based on user settings)
  • Allow users to upload photos (drag and drop) and post their progress with 'blog' post.
  • Provide dashboard feed of what bucket list items friends are checking off and events to participate in.
  • Provide message feed; similar to slack for Bucket List Item subscribers.
  • Enable User Profiles and Public/Private profile settings
  • Enable ability to report Offensive or inappropriate content
  • Ability to set Bucket list items Public or Private
  • Implement date picker from Airbnb library, react-dates, for users to have an actionable plan towards their goals with a deadline.
  • Provide recommendations
